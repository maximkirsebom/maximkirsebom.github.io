My main research interests are:
  • Flows and random walks on homogeneous spaces.
  • Extreme value theory in dynamics, including shrinking target problems and dynamical Borel-Cantelli Lemmas.
  • Applications to Diophantine approximation.
  • Statistical limit theorems in dynamical systems.
  • Random fractals generated by weakly dependent processes.
